My Story

Passionate About Plants & People

Hello! I'm a private gardener based in North Cornwall, with a genuine love of the outdoors and a deep passion for creating beautiful, thriving gardens. I've been working in and around Wadebridge, Polzeath, Rock and the surrounding villages for over fifteen years, building lasting relationships with homeowners who want reliable, high-quality garden care they can trust.

Growing up in Cornwall, I was surrounded by extraordinary coastal and rural landscapes, which sparked my interest in horticulture from an early age. After school I trained formally in horticulture, gaining hands-on experience across ornamental, domestic and commercial gardens throughout the South West. I hold a City & Guilds qualification in Horticulture and have completed specialist training in exotic and architectural planting design.

I work alone, which means you always get me — not a subcontractor. Every garden receives my full attention, and I take pride in leaving each one looking its absolute best.

Core Skills

✂️ Hedgecutting — all species, sizes and styles
🌿 Lawn Mowing & Edging — regular programmes or one-off cuts
🌸 Bed Maintenance — weeding, mulching, deadheading, planting
🌾 General Garden Tidying — seasonal clear-ups, leaf clearing, pruning

🌴 Specialism: Exotic Plantings

Cornwall's mild, moist climate is uniquely suited to exotic and architectural plants — and this is where my real passion lies. I design and maintain planting schemes featuring palms (Trachycarpus, Chamaerops), tree ferns (Dicksonia antarctica), bananas (Musa & Ensete), giant gunnera, and a wide range of Mediterranean and subtropical species. If you've always dreamed of a lush, tropical-feeling Cornish garden, let's make it happen.
